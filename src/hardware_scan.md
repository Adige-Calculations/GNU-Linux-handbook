# Hardware scan

lscpu **Specs of your CPU**

uname -m **Return the architecture of the CPU**

## Bluetooth peripherical installation

To connect a Bluetooth device, do:

sudo bluetoothctl

Then

scan on

Take note of the device you would like to pair

scan off

pair \<**deviceCode**\>

exit
